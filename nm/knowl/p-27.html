Zeros and ones are special kinds of matrices and vectors, filled with the same number: 0 or 1. The commands are called zeros and ones. They require two parameters: the number of rows and the number of columns. For example, zeros(2, 2) creates a 2Ã—2 matrix filled with zeros, and ones(1, 5) is a row vector with 5 entries: [1 1 1 1 1]. These are useful as starting points for building matrices and vectors.
